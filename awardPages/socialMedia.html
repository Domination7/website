Social Media Award

Click here to fill out the application

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the Social Media Award, you must complete 4 of the following challenges:

  • Join your National Officer Team as they host a Tweet-A-Thon during the months of October and January, Using #BPAAllDay
  • Participate in Chapter Social Week in November, either as a chapter or as an individual. The challenges will be listed on BPA social media sites. Once you complete challenges, you will document them and submit your video or photos to Facebook or Twitter using #BPASocial
  • Participate in the BPA Week Challenge. There will be a new challenge every day beginning the second full week in February. Once you complete the challenges, you will document them and submit your video or photos to Facebook or Twitter using the #BPAWeekChallenge
  • Participate in #BPAMillion by posting your favorite memory of BPA with #BPAMillion and tagging Business Professionals of America
  • Establish a committee to complete the requirements of the Social Media Etiquette course sponsored by Social Assurity. This committee should have a presentation that explains social media etiquette, how to be conscious of social media's impact, and how to stay professional on social media
  • Establish a committee to complete the requirements of the Social Media Essentials and College Admissions for Business Professionals of America course sponsored by Social Assurity. This committee should have a presentation that explains social media etiquette, how to be conscious of social media's impact, and how to stay professional on social media

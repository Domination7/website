Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the Special Olympics Award, you must meet these requirements:

  • No previous conduct infractions
  • Open to all levels of BPA (Middle through Post-Secondary)
  • Have either completed 100 service hours with the Special Olympics, or donated $500 to the Special Olympics

Student of the Year Award

Applications to be submitted through official application forms

Purpose

To recognize those students who have made significant contributions to Business Professionals of America as a student member.

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for this award, you have to meet all of the following criteria:

  • Complete at least one full year as a student member
  • Serve as a local, regional, or state officer
  • Attend at least one State Leadership Conference and one National Leadership Conference (Required for Secondary)
  • Compete in the Workplace Skills Assessment Program
  • Earn the Ambassador Torch Award
  • Participate in the BPA Cares Program

You must also meet at least five of the following criteria:

  • Attend at least one State Leadership Conference and one National Leadership Conference (Optional for Post-Secondary)
  • Earn the National Merit Scholar Award
  • Participate in the Intern Program at the National Leadership Conference
  • Participate in the Leadership Academy at the National Leadership Conference
  • Made significant contributions to Special Olympics
  • Earn the President's Volunteer Service Award
  • Completed the Student Member Certification Series
  • Earn the BPA Cares Member Recruiter Award
  • Compete the Life Skills Achievement Program

What to Submit

You must submit the following with your application:

  • A Nomination Form
  • Narrative review of applicant's supportive contributions to BPA on the local, regional, state or national level
  • Two letters of support for the applicant
  • One letter must be from a local advisor
  • One letter must be from the state advisor
  • Resume for the applicant

Recognition

A maximum of one (1) individual from the Secondary and one (1) individual from the Post-Secondary Division will be recognized with a plaque on stage at the National Leadership Conference. Each recipient will also receive a $1,000 scholarship.

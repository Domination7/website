Membership Explosion Award

No application, automatically given

Purpose

To encourage local chapters to become actively involved in the National Officer Team Social Media Challenges; and to encourage local chapters to publicize their local activities and events.

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for this award, you must meet the following criteria:

  • At least 15 registered members
  • At least a 30% increase in membership from the previous year

Recognition

Chapters that achieve the minimum will receive a Certificate of Achievement

The top chapter in each division with the highest number of new members will receive an Award of Achievement and recognition on-stage at the National Leadership Conference

The top chapter in each division with the highest percentage growth of new members will recieve an Award of Achievement and recognition on-stage at the National Leadership Conference

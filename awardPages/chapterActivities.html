Chapter Activities Award of Excellence

Click here to fill out the application

Purpose

To recognize local chapters for their outstanding activities and program of work and to motivate all local chapters to strive for an outstanding program of activities.

Required Activities

To be eligible for the Chapter Activities Award of Excellence, you must submit these awards:

  • Marketing & Public Relations Award with one qualifying activity
  • Community Service Award with one activity, either totaling 100 hours or $500 donated.

Activity descriptions for at least one of the following awards:

  • Special Olympics with at least one activity.
  • Safety Awareness with at least one activity.
  • Environmental Action/Awareness with at least one activity.

An activity description relating to each of the following activities:

  • Local officer installation
  • Local officer training
  • A BPA Leadership Conference attended by your chapter (regional, state, or national)
  • Chapter social activity
  • Local chapter budget
  • A career development related activity for the chapter (could include seeing a speaker, watching a film, taking a field trip, etc.)

Recognition

Chapter Activities Award of Excellence: A plaque will be presented to each qualifying chapter on stage at the National Leadership Conference.

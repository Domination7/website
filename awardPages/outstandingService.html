Eligibility Requirements

To nominate someone for this award, the nominee must not have received this award within five years and you must have completed the following:

  • The nomination form
  • List of national activities and involvement
  • List of state and local activities and involvement
  • List of participation in other professional organizations and community service
  • List of any other pertinent information about the nominee that you believe will assist the Committee in making the selections
  • National staff and current student members are ineligble for this award

Recognition

A maximum of 5 individuals will be recognized with a plaque at the National Leadership Conference

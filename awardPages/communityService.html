Community Service Award

Click here to fill out the application

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the Community Service Award, you must meet these requirements:

  • No previous conduct infractions
  • Open to all levels of BPA (Middle through Post-Secondary)
  • Have either completed 100 service hours, or donated $500 to charitable organizations

Application Requirements

If you meet the eligibilty requirements, then you are able to submit an application. These are the requirements when submit the application on the form above :

  • Activities participated in from April 2, 2017 to April 1, 2018 will be counted
  • No description longer than 500 words
  • To include an activity, you must report what you did, the organization you volunteered with, the date that you completed the activity, and where this took place.
